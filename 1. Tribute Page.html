Here's a timeline of Heath Ledger's life:


  • April 4, 1979 - Heath Andrew Ledger born to parents Sally Ledger Bell, a French teacher, and Kim Ledger, a racing car driver and mining engineer in Perth, Western Australia.
  • 1992 - Scores his first acting role in Australian film Clowning Around.
  • 1997 - Lands a part in Australian film Blackrock and appears on television soap Home and Away.
  • 1997 - In TV series Roar.
  • 1998 - Lands small part in Australian movie Paws.
  • 1999 - Stars in Two Hands with Bryan Brown and Hollywood teen film 10 Things I Hate About You.
  • 2000 - Stars in The Patriot with Mel Gibson.
  • 2001 - Releases films Monster's Ball and A Knight's Tale.
  • 2002 - Stars in The Four Feathers.
  • 2003 - Stars in The Order, Ned Kelly.
  • 2004 - Ledger begins dating Michelle Williams, whom he met on the set of Brokeback Mountain.
  • 2006 - Ledger is nominated for a best actor Academy Award for Brokeback Mountain, but misses out.
  • 2006 - Ledger Returns to Australia to star in Candy with Abbie Cornish and Geoffrey Rush.
  • 2007 - Ledger is one of six actors to portray Bob Dylan in I'm Not There. He films The Dark Knight, in which he plays the Joker.
  • September 2007 - Ledger splits with actress partner Michelle Williams.
  • January 22, 2008 - Ledger found dead in Manhattan apartment aged 28 in what authorities say is a possible drug-related death.
